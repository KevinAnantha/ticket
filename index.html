Stunning View

At 3,145 meters, the summit provides a panoramic view of nearby volcanoes like Merapi, Sindoro, Sumbing, and Lawu. The vast grasslands make it one of the most scenic hikes in Indonesia.

Eternal Beauty

Edelweiss (Anaphalis javanica), often called the "Flower of Immortality," thrives on Merbabu’s high-altitude slopes and is a protected species in Indonesia.

Sun and Star

The trek provides a breathtaking views, especially on golden hour and also incredible stargazing experience. If you are lucky enough, you may capture the milky way!

Challenging Yet Rewarding

Merbabu has multiple trails that range from moderate to challenging. The hike is suitable for both intermediate and experienced hikers, with well-marked trails that offer a good balance of endurance and adventure.
